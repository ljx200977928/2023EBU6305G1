Great Gate of Great Wall

The Dajingmen Section of the Great Wall was first built in 1485, the 21st year of Chenghua in the Ming Dynasty, on the basis of the Great Wall of the Northern Wei Dynasty, which has a history of more than 500 years.

Xuanhua District ancient town Shuo Lou

Zhenshuo building was built in the orthodox five years of the Ming Dynasty (1440), for the double eaves nine ridges of the mountain top of the tall building, two floors of 15 meters high, very magnificent, for the national key cultural relic protection units.

Qiaoxi District

Qinghe Film and Television Industry Base is a key cultural project in Zhangjiakou and a preheating project for the Winter Olympics. The base covers a total area of 28.2 square kilometers.

Wanlong Ski Resort and National Ski Jumping Center in Chongli District

Chongli Wanlong Ski Resort is located in Honghuliang, Chongli District, Zhangjiakou City, Hebei Province. It covers an area of more than 30 square kilometers. The highest point is 2110.3 meters above sea level and the vertical drop is 550 meters.

The National Ski Jumping Center is the first ski jumping venue in China. It is one of the competition venues with the largest amount of engineering and the highest technical difficulty in Chongli Competition area. The shape of the venue fits with the traditional Chinese ornament "Ruyi", which symbolizes good luck and ruyi and is called "Snow Ruyi".

Zhangbei County

Grassland

Grassland Tianlu is an important passage connecting Chongli Ski Area, Chicheng Hot Spring Area, Zhangbei Grassland Scenic spot, Bailongdong Scenic Spot and Daqingshan Scenic Spot. It is also one of the ten most beautiful roads in China.

Yuanzhong Capital ruins

The capital of Yuan Zhong was founded in the eleventh year of Yuan Dade (1307), with the name of Yuan Dadu (now Beijing) and Yuan Shangdu (now east of Zhenglan Banner, Inner Mongolia).

Yu County Weizhou ancient city

Weizhou has a long history, has found millions of years ago paleolithic sites and numerous neolithic sites. According to archaeological data, people had settled down in the area of Weizhou city in the Eastern Zhou Dynasty at the latest, so the history of Weizhou city can be more than 2700 years.

The ancient town of Nuanquan is known as "Nuanquan" because it has a spring with the same water temperature all the year round. It is a typical Ming Dynasty citadel, known as "the first fort of Chinese folk custom".The highlight of the town is the Dashuhua show, which has a history of more than 300 years.

Known as the "Roof of North China" and the highest peak in Hebei Province, it is a paradise for hiking enthusiasts.

The Jade Emperor Pavilion, also known as "Jingbian Tower", was built in the tenth year of Hongwu in the Ming Dynasty (1377 AD). It was a temple dedicated to the Jade Emperor and had the function of north defense of the ancient city of Weizhou.
