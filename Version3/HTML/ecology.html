E-commerce Devleopemnt

Yu County, Zhangjiakou City, Hebei Province vigorously develops e-commerce, builds beautiful villages, and promotes the development of green and organic agriculture, aiming to promote rural economic development and ecological civilization construction. As the place where Yuan Longping, the father of Chinese agronomy, demonstrated thousands of acres of land, Yu County has unique natural and human resources for the development of green and organic agriculture. At the same time, the county is also actively playing the role of e-commerce in rural revitalization, promoting the circulation and economic benefits of agricultural products through online sales.

Green chemical industrial Park

The establishment of a green chemical industrial park in Chongli District, Zhangjiakou is one of the measures taken by the Zhangjiakou Municipal Government to promote green development, and it is also part of the construction of an ecologically civilized city. The industrial park will be committed to creating an environmentally friendly chemical industry, and realize a circular economy through the collection and utilization of waste water. This model can not only create new economic value, but also help protect the environment and promote sustainable development. As a famous tourist destination, Chongli District is particularly important for environmental protection. The discharge of chemical wastewater is an important part of environmental protection issues, and by establishing a green chemical industrial park, the wastewater can be recycled and the impact on the environment can be reduced.

Natural Resource Protection

Zhangjiakou has many beautiful natural landscapes and protected areas. The local government has strengthened the protection of these natural resources and established a series of protection systems, such as ecological restoration, water source protection to ensure the integrity and aesthetics of the natural ecology.The government proposes to build "Ecological Zhangjiakou, Green Economy", promote construction of an ecological city from various aspects such as urban planning, construction, and management, and accelerate the pace of coordination between ecological civilization and economic development.

"Three North" shelter forest

Play an important role in ensuring the safety of the capital's water resources and ecological environment. Since the reform and opening up, Zhangjiakou has successively implemented a series of national ecological construction projects such as the "Three North" shelterbelts, Beijing-Tianjin sandstorm source control, and returning farmland to forests and grasslands, and the phenomenon of land desertification has been initially curbed.

Air Pollution Prevention

Zhangjiakou seized the favorable opportunity of joining hands with Beijing to be shortlisted for the 2022 Winter Olympics and the coordinated development of the integrated Beijing-Tianjin-Hebei region, making efforts to improve the ecological environment as the lifeline of its own development, taking air pollution control and improving air quality as adjustments to the structure, An important starting point for benefiting people's livelihood, all departments at all levels unify their thinking, work together to tackle difficulties, and solidly promote the prevention and control of air pollution.

Water Pollution Prevention

In recent years, Zhangjiakou City has focused on the quality of the water environment to fight the battle of pollution prevention and control. It insists on both pollution reduction and ecological expansion, coordinates water environment, water ecology, water resources and other elements, and highlights precise and scientific pollution control. , Pollution control according to the law, strengthen the comprehensive treatment of drinking water, industrial and domestic sewage, black and odorous water bodies, rivers, lakes and reservoirs, implement precise policies, continuously improve the modernization level of the governance system and governance capabilities, and continue to fight the battle for clear water.

Grassland Road Prevention

Guanting Reservoir National Wetland Park

Overlooking Zhangjiakou Huailai Guanting Reservoir National Wetland Park from the air, the green mountains and green waters intertwined with the blue sky, chirping birds can be heard endlessly, and a beautiful picture of the harmonious coexistence of man and nature unfolds. This wetland park spans the central part of Huailai County, Zhangjiakou City, and is dominated by the floodplain of the Yongding River and the area below 479 meters of the Guanting Reservoir. It is the largest national wetland park in North China. It is the brightest pearl in the construction process of the conservation function area and the ecological environment support area. Strolling through the Guanting Reservoir National Wetland Park, you can see winding streams, green mirror lakes, and blue waves and silver beaches. After several years of transformation, this place has changed from a polluted beach to an ecological business card of Huailai County, promoting the cultural development of Huailai County. The tourism industry is further optimized and developed.
