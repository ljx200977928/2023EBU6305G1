Zhangbei and Kangbao County

Zhangbei County, Zhangjiakou City, Hebei Province, is located in the northwestern part of Hebei Province, the southern edge of the Inner Mongolia Plateau in Bashang area, border area 109 kilometers east and west. Zhangbei County is the closest plateau area to Beijing and Tianjin. It has a cool climate in summer and is a famous summer resort around Beijing and Tianjin.

Kangbao County belongs to Zhangjiakou City, located in the northwest of Hebei Province. In the east, north and west, Taiwusi Banner, Zhengxiangbai Banner, Huade County and Shangdu County of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region borders, and Zhangbei, Guyuan and Shangyi counties are adjacent to the south. The total area of the county is 3,365 square kilometers. Kangbao County is the hometown of Chinese folk culture and art and the hometown of Chinese duo Tai art

Guyuan and Shangyi County

Guyuan County is located in the north of Hebei Province, the upper reaches of Lightning River, near the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, under the jurisdiction of Zhangjiakou City, the total land area of 3601 square kilometers, the county government is based in Pingdingbao Town. Guyuan County has a long history. It has been inhabited since the Neolithic Age about 6,000 years ago.

Shangyi County was established in 1936 and named Shangyi County by "advocating rites and justice". Shangyi County has numerous rivers and moorlands, with the largest area of North China's Chahan Nur wetland, Daqingshan Forest Park National Forest Park, Shirenbei Geopark, Yuanyang Lake International Camping Park and other scenic spots.

Huaian and Zhuolu County

Huaian County, affiliated to Zhangjiakou City of Hebei Province, is located in the northwest of Hebei Province, at the junction of Shanxi, Hebei and Mongolia three provinces (regions), located at the intersection of the Beijing-Tianjin-Hebei metropolitan circle and the Shanxi, Hebei and Mongolia economic circle, known as the "Golden Triangle".

Zhuolu County is affiliated to Zhangjiakou City of Hebei Province, located in the northwest of Hebei Province, the lower reaches of Sanggan River, and Xihuayuan District of Zhangjiakou City and the suburbs of Beijing, with a total area of 2802 square kilometers. Famous scenic spots are Yellow Emperor Spring, Yellow Emperor City site, Chinese Hefu Altar, Chinese Three Ancestral Hall, Xuanyuan Lake.

Chicheng and Huailai County

Chicheng County is located in the east of Zhangjiakou City, Hebei Province, the upper reaches of the Baihe River, east of Beijing, north of Bashang grassland, has the reputation of "outside the pearl" and "North of Beijing Water city". Chicheng County is one of the 14 counties (cities) around the capital Green Economy Zone in Hebei Province.

Huailai is one of the first grain base counties and "double ring" vegetable basket bases in Hebei Province, the hometown of grape and wine in China, the important water source of the capital and the hometown of the hero Dong Cunrui.
