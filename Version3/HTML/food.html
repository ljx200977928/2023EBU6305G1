Sesame sauce baked cakes

One of the most common breakfasts, a mouthful full of fragrant wood. The essential staple food after shabu mutton and hot pot is woody, I like to eat layer by layer, first eat all the sesame seeds, especially fragrant, and then squeeze the vegetables, especially enjoy!

Chinese cabbage boiled yam

Tender cabbage with soft potatoes, boiled rotten paste, add a little chili oil not to mention how fragrant, I eat a big plate at a time! Boiling vegetables is the great love of Zhangjiakou people, more retains the taste of the dish itself, less oil, and easy to digest!

Charcoal kebabs

Seeing the barbecue stalls on the streets and alleys every summer, you know that the lamb kebabs in Zhangjiakou are popular, the key is that our lamb is good, the little sheep on the grassland on the dam! In addition to lamb kebabs, there are also lamb loins and lamb liver.

Sumena

There are many ways to eat, Sumen wowo, Sumen fish, cold mixed noodles, fried noodles, Sumen noodles to take the cake sea, Sumen wo to mix a variety of boiled vegetables to eat, or dip in mutton soup to eat, eat can't stop, but can be leisurely, Sumen noodles are not easy to digest, you can eat less at night!

Stir-fried fish with noodles

Each family has different recipes, but I think that no matter how you stir-fry, you put less vinegar and add some chili oil, which is a delicious taste. The biggest feature of the fish is that it has a chewy head, which fully shows the taste and aroma of the noodles!

Chaigou Fort bacon

That's definitely famous, using good pork belly flowers, fat but not greasy! Excellent appetizers! However, my favorite is the bacon spring cake, sliced bacon, finely cut cucumber, shredded green onion, made into a bowl of sweet noodle sauce, served with thin spring cakes, rolled and eaten!

Zhangjiakou hands on meat

This used to be the famous food of the Qing royal mansion, also called "hand steak meat", optional lamb chops and lamb leg, the former fat and lean, the latter pure lean meat, fat but not greasy, no smell, you can also eat dipping sauce, leek flowers, garlic paste soy sauce, depending on your taste.
